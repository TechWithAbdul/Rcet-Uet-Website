Mechanical Engineering

Electrical Engineering

The Electrical Engineering Program at Rachna College of Engineering & Technology, Gujranwala is one of the most popular Engineering Program of the region. The Electrical Engineering Department at RCET has continued this legacy ever since its establishment in 2003.

OUR FACILITIES

Largest Play Ground

The RCET provides facilities to the students for participation in games and sports, both outdoors and indoors. Facilities are provided for all the major sports including cricket, Hockey, foot Ball, tennis, badminton, basketball, table Tennis, Body Building and athletics.

Library

It has four main sections i.e. Circulation, Reference, Book Bank and Serial section. Currently it has seating capacity of about 40 readers. It has significantly improved its book holding and it is going through an ambitious program of computerization of its services and operation. It is a lending library and most of its holdings are available to members on loan. The library holdings can be browsed through an Internet based fully searchable catalogue. In the expansion plan there is a dedicated building for the library.

Mosque

To provide the prayers facility to the students and staff, a beautiful mosque is situated at midway of the Staff Colony and the hostel.
